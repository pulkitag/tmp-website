Research

The overarching research interest is to build machines that can automatically and continously learn about their environment. The hope is that the end result of such learning will be similar to what we might call as common sense. I call this line of work as computational sensorimotor learning and it encompasses robotics, reinforcement learning, computer vision and other learning based approaches to control. Some of my past work has also touched upon cognitive science, neuroscience and draw inspiration from these discplines. My representative papers are highlighted.
